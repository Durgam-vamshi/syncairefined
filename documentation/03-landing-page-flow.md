# Landing Page Flow

The landing page is intentionally designed as a product experience, not a consulting page. It blends narrative blocks with catalog shelves to feel like a Play Store while still telling a clear adoption story.

Key structural ideas:
- A shelf is a container listing AI agents based on a specific criteria.
- There are many shelves to reinforce breadth. The page originally had around 20 shelves and was reduced, but it is still intentionally dense.
- Narrative blocks are interleaved with shelves to share our point of view while showing the catalog in action.

Flow summary:
1) Top Navigation
   - Minimal header with the brand plus four primary actions: All-Access, Enterprise Integration, AI Workshops/Training, Contact.

2) Hero and All-Access
   - Sets the promise: immediate access to agents at enterprise scale.
   - Anchors the $1,000 per month All-Access path and the knowledge transfer signal.

3) Category Filters
   - Offers quick segmentation by workflow type.

4) Narrative Sections (Sticky Story + Scrolling Shelves)
   - Five phases with supporting shelves, interactive panels, and proof artifacts.
   - Sections 3 and 5 use a Z-layout (narrative on the right, shelves on the left).

5) Closing CTA
   - Simple two-action close: All-Access and Contact.

Overall, the page alternates narrative depth with catalog breadth, creating a lively discovery flow while still communicating what we do as a company.
