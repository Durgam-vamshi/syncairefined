# Design Evolution and Current State

This section summarizes the high-level design decisions and what we have implemented so far.

Product and narrative direction:
- We are enterprise integration consultants, but we present the offering as a Play Store experience rather than a traditional services page.
- The UI is intentionally lively and dense, with shelves of AI agents to convey breadth and discovery.
- Narrative blocks are interleaved with shelves to explain the adoption philosophy, pricing model, and training posture.
- The right column balances controlled density using a live ticker, tabbed stack viewer, and a drag-to-scroll crate.

Design changes completed:
- Pirate Wires editorial style: serif narrative voice + monospace data stream.
- Deep-void dark theme with monochrome palette and sharp 1px borders.
- Hyprland-style blur applied only to key layers (topbar, sticky narrative, speaker dossier), with a cyborg wallpaper inside the blur to make depth visible.
- Z-layout applied to alternating sections so the narrative column switches sides.
- All-Access CTA elevated in the header with a white gradient and subtle green glow.

Current direction:
- Dark monochrome with controlled accent glow.
- Editorial catalog experience with sharp hierarchy and minimal rounded corners.
- Performance-conscious visuals that still feel premium and cinematic.

If needed, we can continue refining density, narrative clarity, or performance without changing the structure of the page.
